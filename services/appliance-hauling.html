APPLIANCE REMOVAL — Q&A

What appliances do you haul?

We remove all major household appliances: refrigerators, freezers, washers, dryers, dishwashers, stoves, ovens, microwaves, water heaters, air conditioners, and more. If it plugs in or runs on gas, we can haul it.

Do I need to disconnect the appliance first?

For most appliances — yes, please disconnect power and water before we arrive. For gas appliances like stoves, please have a licensed professional disconnect the gas line first for safety. We'll handle everything else from there.

Where do old appliances go after you haul them?

We prioritize responsible disposal. Working appliances may be donated. Non-working units are taken to certified recycling facilities where metals and components are properly processed. We don't just dump and drive.

How much does appliance hauling cost in Bakersfield?

Single appliance pickup typically runs $75–$150 depending on size, weight, and access. Multiple appliances get a bulk rate. Text us a photo for a fast, no-obligation estimate.

Can you haul from upstairs or tight spaces?

Yes. We regularly handle appliance removals from upper floors, narrow hallways, and tight utility closets. We bring the equipment needed and take precautions to protect your walls and floors.

Do you haul refrigerators with freon?

Yes. Refrigerators with freon are handled in compliance with EPA guidelines. They are delivered to certified facilities for proper refrigerant recovery before metal recycling.
