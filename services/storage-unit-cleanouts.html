STORAGE CLEANOUT — Q&A

Do you work directly with storage facility managers?

Yes. We work with facility managers to clear abandoned or delinquent units quickly. We're professional, reliable, and can be scheduled around the facility's access hours. Call us to set up a standing relationship for recurring hauls.

How quickly can you clear a storage unit?

Most standard units (5x10 to 10x20) can be cleared in 1–2 hours. Larger or heavily packed units may take 2–4 hours. We can usually schedule same-day or next-morning for Bakersfield locations.

What do you do with the items from a storage unit?

Usable items may be taken to donation centers. Metal goes to recycling. Everything else is properly disposed of at licensed facilities. We don't dump illegally and we document hauls when needed.

How is storage cleanout pricing calculated?

Pricing is based on unit size and volume. A 5x10 unit typically runs $100–$200. A 10x20 runs $250–$400. Full truck-load pricing applies for larger volumes. We confirm price before starting.

Can you haul multiple units in one visit?

Yes. For facilities needing multiple units cleared at once, we can bring an additional truck or schedule back-to-back hauls. Volume pricing applies. Contact us to coordinate.

Do you handle climate-controlled or high-value storage contents?

We treat all storage contents with care. If there may be valuable items in the unit, we'll flag them before removal. We only remove what you explicitly authorize us to haul.
