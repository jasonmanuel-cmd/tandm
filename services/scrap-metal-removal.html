SCRAP METAL — Q&A

What types of scrap metal do you haul?

We haul all types of scrap metal including steel, iron, aluminum, copper, and mixed metal. This includes appliances, car parts, old fencing, gates, pipes, structural steel, bed frames, exercise equipment, and bulk metal accumulation.

Do you offer payment for scrap metal?

In some cases, high-value metal loads may qualify for a reduced haul fee or offset. This depends on the type, quantity, and current recycling market. Contact us with details and we'll give you an honest breakdown.

Can you haul large structural metal pieces?

Yes. We handle large structural steel, I-beams, and heavy scrap loads. For oversized pieces, we may need to pre-cut or section them. Let us know what you're working with and we'll plan accordingly.

Do you remove old chain link or wood fencing?

Yes. Fencing removal and haul-away is a common job for us. We remove metal chain link, wrought iron, wood, and composite fencing — posts and all.

How is scrap metal removal priced?

Pricing is based on volume and the labor required to load it. Heavier loads in accessible areas are straightforward to quote. Text us photos and we'll give you a price within minutes.

Do you serve commercial properties and job sites?

Yes. Contractors, fabricators, and commercial properties regularly use our metal haul services. We work fast and can schedule recurring pickups for ongoing projects that generate metal waste.
