HOARDER CLEANOUT — Q&A

How do you approach hoarder cleanouts with respect?

We come in without judgment and follow your direction entirely. We work at a pace you're comfortable with, and we never remove anything without your explicit say-so. Our goal is to restore the space while honoring the process.

Can you handle a full house with years of accumulation?

Yes. We've cleared properties with extreme accumulation across multiple rooms. We plan multiple truck loads when needed and coordinate the project from start to finish. No job is too large for our team.

Do you help sort items that might be kept or donated?

We can assist with sorting into "keep," "donate," and "haul" piles at your request. We partner with local donation centers and will make a drop-off run before taking the rest to disposal.

How is a hoarder cleanout priced?

Pricing depends on volume, number of truck loads, and access complexity. For these larger projects, we typically do a walkthrough or photo review first and provide a flat project rate. We confirm pricing before any work begins.

How long does a hoarder house cleanout take?

It varies significantly based on property size and accumulation level. A moderate single-story home typically takes 1–2 days. Severe accumulation in larger homes may require 3+ days. We'll give you an honest timeline upfront.

Do you coordinate with property managers or social services?

Yes. We regularly work alongside property managers, family representatives, and in some cases, social workers to ensure the cleanout meets everyone's needs and deadlines.
