MATTRESS DISPOSAL — Q&A

How much does mattress pickup cost in Bakersfield?

A single mattress pickup typically runs $50–$80. Box springs and bed frames are often added for a small additional fee. For bulk pickups (4+ units), we offer landlord rates — call or text for a custom quote.

Do you pick up mattresses from upstairs bedrooms?

Yes. We carry mattresses down stairs and out of tight hallways. Just let us know at booking so we can confirm access and route the mattress safely without damaging your home.

Do you also take box springs and bed frames?

Absolutely. Box springs, metal bed frames, wooden headboards, and bed bases are all items we haul. Bundle them together for a combined price.

How do you dispose of mattresses responsibly?

We take mattresses to certified disposal and recycling facilities. In Kern County, many mattress components (foam, springs, fabric) can be recycled. We comply with California mattress disposal regulations.

Do you offer mattress removal for landlords and property managers?

Yes — this is one of our most common services. Tenant turnover often leaves behind multiple mattresses. We offer priority scheduling and bulk rates for property managers who need a reliable haul partner.

Can I get same-day mattress pickup?

Often yes, depending on our route that day. Text us your location and the number of mattresses early in the morning for the best same-day availability.
