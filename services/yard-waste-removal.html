YARD WASTE REMOVAL — Q&A

What types of yard waste do you haul?

We haul tree trimmings, branches, palm fronds, sod, dirt, gravel, leaves, lawn debris, fencing materials, and general yard waste. If it came from your yard and can be loaded in a truck, we take it.

Do you haul dirt and gravel?

Yes. Dirt and gravel hauls are priced by volume and weight since they're heavy loads. Text us the approximate amount (cubic yards or load size) for an accurate quote before scheduling.

Do I need to bag or bundle yard waste before you arrive?

Not necessarily. Loose branches and debris can be loaded directly. However, having piles consolidated in one area of the yard helps us work faster and may reduce your cost.

How much does yard waste removal cost in Bakersfield?

Pricing typically runs $100–$300 depending on volume. Large loads or multiple truck runs will be priced accordingly. We always confirm pricing before we start loading.

Do you work with landscapers and tree trimming companies?

Yes. We provide haul-away services for landscaping crews who generate large amounts of green waste. We offer recurring haul partnerships for landscaping companies that need reliable disposal after big jobs.

Where does yard waste go after pickup?

Green waste (branches, leaves, grass, sod) goes to certified composting and green waste facilities in Kern County. Dirt and inert materials go to licensed disposal sites. We don't dump illegally.
