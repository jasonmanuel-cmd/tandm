JUNK REMOVAL — Q&A

What items do you haul in Bakersfield?

We haul furniture, sofas, chairs, tables, dressers, mattresses, appliances, electronics, clothing, boxes, garage contents, attic cleanouts, and general household clutter. We don't haul hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, asbestos, or biohazardous waste.

How is junk removal priced?

We price by truck space — the more room your items take up, the more it costs. A single item pickup starts around $75. A full truck load ranges $300–$600 depending on weight. We confirm the exact price before loading anything.

Do I need to carry anything to the curb?

No. We come to the item, wherever it is — inside a room, in the basement, in the attic. Just show us what goes and we handle all the heavy lifting and carrying.

Can you do same-day junk removal in Bakersfield?

Often yes. Text us before noon for the best chance at same-day service. We route through most Bakersfield neighborhoods daily and can frequently add last-minute stops when the load allows.

Do you recycle or donate any of the junk you haul?

Yes. Usable furniture and goods may be taken to local donation centers. Metal items go to recycling facilities. We keep landfill use to a minimum wherever possible.

Do you serve all Bakersfield neighborhoods?

Yes — we operate across all of Kern County. Seven Oaks, Rosedale, Oildale, East Bakersfield, Stockdale, Downtown, Westchester, and everywhere else. We're local and we know the routes.
