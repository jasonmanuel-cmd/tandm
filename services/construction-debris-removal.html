CONSTRUCTION DEBRIS — Q&A

What construction materials do you haul?

We haul concrete chunks, drywall, roofing shingles, framing lumber, tile, flooring, insulation, fencing, and general demolition debris. If it came off a job site, we can likely take it.

Do you serve contractors and general contractors?

Yes. We work directly with GCs, subcontractors, and owner-builders to fit hauls into project timelines. Recurring haul contracts are available for ongoing projects. Call to discuss volume pricing.

Can you haul concrete and heavy masonry?

Yes, within reason. Concrete is heavy — we price it by weight and volume. For large concrete pads or significant masonry removal, text us details so we can confirm truck capacity and provide accurate pricing.

How quickly can you arrive after a demo?

For most Bakersfield job sites, we can arrive same-day or next morning when scheduled before noon. We're built for speed — debris cleared means your crew can keep moving.

Do you handle hazardous construction materials?

We do not haul hazardous materials including asbestos, lead paint, or chemical waste. For these materials, you'll need a licensed hazmat contractor. We're happy to point you in the right direction.

Do you do site sweeps after the haul?

Every commercial haul includes a final site sweep. We don't leave nails, small debris, or loose material behind. A clean site means a safe site.
