GARAGE CLEAN UP — Q&A

Do you sweep up after the haul?

Absolutely. Every garage project ends with a full broom-sweep. We don't leave until the floor is clear — no loose debris, no leftover cardboard, nothing.

Do I need to sort or bag anything before you arrive?

No prep needed. You point out what goes, we handle the rest. We sort, carry, and load everything ourselves. If you're unsure about an item, just ask and we'll help you decide.

How much does garage clean up cost in Bakersfield?

Pricing is based on how much truck space your items take up. A typical single-car garage haul runs $150–$350 depending on volume and material type. Text us photos for a fast, no-obligation estimate — we usually respond within 10 minutes.

Can you haul large or heavy items like old shelving, freezers, or car parts?

Yes. Our 16ft high-capacity truck handles heavy items including metal shelving units, chest freezers, car batteries, tires, and scrap metal. We bring the muscle — you don't need to move a thing.

How long does a typical garage clean up take?

Most single-car garages are cleared in 1–2 hours. Two-car garages with heavy accumulation typically take 2–3 hours. We move fast and work as a team to minimize your disruption.

Do you serve all Bakersfield neighborhoods?

Yes — we operate across all of Kern County including Seven Oaks, Rosedale, Stockdale, Oildale, East Bakersfield, and everywhere in between. We frequently route through most areas and can often add you to the same-day schedule.
