KNOWLEDGE BASE

FREQUENTLY ASKED

What areas do you serve?

We serve Bakersfield and surrounding Kern County communities, working with homeowners, landlords, realtors, and contractors.

What kind of junk do you haul?

We remove household clutter, furniture, appliances, construction debris, yard waste, storage unit clean-outs, garage clean-outs, and full property clean-ups. We do not accept hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, or waste that requires special handling.

Do you offer residential and commercial services?

Yes. We handle everything from single-item pickups at homes to full clean-outs for rentals, flips, offices, and job sites.

How do I get a quote?

The fastest way is to send us photos of the junk pile or area you want cleared. From those pictures, we can usually give you a fast, no-obligation estimate and available time slots.

Can you do same-day or next-day service?

When our schedule allows, we do our best to offer same-day or next-day pickups. Message us with photos and your location, and we'll let you know the soonest we can be there.

Do I need to move anything before you arrive?

No. Our crew does all the heavy lifting. Just point out what needs to go, and we'll load it, haul it, and clean up the area before we leave.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is typically based on how much space your items take up in our truck, the type of material, and how difficult it is to access. Once we see photos (or the job in person), we'll give you a clear price before we start so there are no surprises.

Do you offer dumpster rental as well as full-service hauling?

Yes, we can provide full-service junk removal where we load everything, or dumpster-style solutions for certain projects so you can load at your own pace and we haul it away when you're done.

What happens to the junk after you haul it?

Whenever possible, we prioritize proper disposal and responsible handling in line with local regulations and best practices for junk removal. Items that can be recycled or diverted from the landfill may be handled through appropriate channels, depending on condition and material type.

Do you haul appliances and heavy debris?

Yes. We haul refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, and similar items, and we take construction debris such as drywall, lumber, and roofing when it fits our load and disposal plan. Ask when you text your photos.
